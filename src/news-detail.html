Showroom open day Cersaie 2017
For Cersaie 2017, Atlas Concorde opened the doors of its Fiorano Modenese headquarters for its traditional Open Day with the participation of numerous national and international partners from more than 50 countries.

The event was also an opportunity to inaugurate the new showroom pavilion that, together with the showroom, completes the companyâ€™s exhibition space. The new pavilion, which is architecturally inspired by winter gardens, is connected by a short path to the indoor showroom, crossing the outdoor area and arriving at the new structure, surrounded by greenery and destined for the presentation of the latest Atlas Concorde products.

During the evening, guests were taken on a dynamic, engaging tour, surrounded by contemporary and multi-faceted interior design projects, concluding the evening with music, entertainment, and an exclusive gala dinner. The Open Day was an opportunity to spend time together in a context that demonstrates the potential of porcelain floors and wall tiles rich in formats, colors, and finishes, suitable for designing interior and exterior spaces with perfect stylistic continuity.
